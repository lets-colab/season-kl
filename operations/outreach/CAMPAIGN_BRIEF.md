# Season KL Outreach Campaign Brief

Update this file before each research run.

## Active campaign

- **Campaign name:** Season Inner Circle — First Chapter
- **Primary objective:** Build a handpicked pool of potential first-time guests and community members for Season KL.
- **Location:** Kuala Lumpur
- **Target group:** Public Instagram profiles with credible KL relevance, recent activity and a natural fit with Season KL's nightlife/community positioning.
- **Research batch:** 50 qualified prospects
- **First-contact batch:** Maximum 20 P1 prospects at a time
- **Human owner:** Dr.X / Season KL community team

## Invitation direction

The invitation should communicate that:
- Season KL is building a private circle that shapes each weekly chapter.
- The person is being approached individually, not added to a mass list.
- The first message should invite interest; detailed requirements or signup links come after a positive reply.

## Exclusions

Do not include:
- private profiles with no public qualification evidence
- profiles with no credible KL connection
- inactive or abandoned accounts
- obvious business-only accounts unless the campaign specifically asks for them
- minors or profiles whose age appears uncertain
- anyone previously marked `DO_NOT_CONTACT`

## Information still required for each new weekly run

- event date
- event theme
- guest benefit/offer
- eligibility requirements
- signup or RSVP link
- final DM wording

Until these are confirmed, research can proceed, but the agent must not mark prospects beyond `APPROVED`.
